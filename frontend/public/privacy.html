Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

GDPR CCPA No cookies No tracking

Schengenerator is a free, open-source tool that calculates your Schengen Area day count from a Google Maps Timeline export. This policy explains exactly how your data is handled.

The short version

Your location data is processed entirely in-memory on Cloudflare's edge infrastructure and is never stored, logged, or retained in any form. We collect no personal data. There are no cookies, no analytics trackers, and no third-party scripts.

What happens when you upload a file

  • Your Timeline JSON file is read locally in your browser and sent via an encrypted HTTPS request to our analysis endpoint.
  • The endpoint runs on a stateless Cloudflare Worker. It parses the file in memory, computes your Schengen day count, and returns the result as JSON.
  • The file and all GPS coordinates are discarded immediately after the response is sent. Nothing is written to disk, a database, or any storage layer.
  • The result is held in your browser's memory only. Closing or refreshing the page clears it entirely.

Data we do not collect

  • GPS coordinates or location history
  • Names, email addresses, or any identity information
  • Device fingerprints or advertising identifiers
  • Usage behavior or analytics events

Infrastructure & third parties

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. As with any CDN, Cloudflare's infrastructure retains standard edge logs (IP address, timestamp, HTTP status, bytes transferred) for security and abuse prevention purposes. These logs do not contain the contents of your uploaded file. Cloudflare's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services, SDKs, or tracking pixels are loaded by this application.

Cookies & local storage

Schengenerator sets no cookies and writes nothing to localStorage or sessionStorage. Your browser may cache static assets (CSS, JS) in its normal HTTP cache; this contains no personal data.

GDPR (EU/EEA users)

Because Schengenerator does not collect, store, or process any personal data in a persistent form, it does not act as a data controller or data processor under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). No lawful basis for processing is required as no personal data is retained. There is no data to access, correct, export, or erase.

CCPA (California residents)

Schengenerator does not sell, share, or disclose personal information to third parties. No personal information is collected for business or commercial purposes. California residents have no additional obligations under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) because no personal data is held.

Children

Schengenerator does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (US) or under 16 (EU). Because no personal data is collected at all, this is satisfied by design.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to how data is handled, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date at the top will change. Substantive changes will also be noted in the GitHub commit history.
